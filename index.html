GIS Toolkit for Bhotiya Cultural Routes
2023 – 2025 · IIT Roorkee · DST-funded
Arches · FAIR
I deployed an open-access Arches Heritage Inventory and Management System to document Bhotiya cultural routes in Uttarakhand — integrating spatial, semantic and narrative data into a coherent knowledge graph. This involved designing resource models, aligning entities with CIDOC-CRM, and managing multi-source dataset ingestion, from field surveys to existing GIS layers. The toolkit enables planners, researchers and communities to explore routes, rituals and sites through an interactive, map-based interface.
Arches PostGIS Cultural routes
3D Reconstruction of Endangered Wooden Temples
2024 – 2025 · IIT Roorkee · Oxford Brookes-funded
Photogrammetry & ALS
As field lead, I designed and executed a workflow combining UAV photogrammetry, aerial laser scanning (ALS) and RTK-based control to generate high-fidelity 3D models of historic wooden temples. I coordinated acquisitions, managed data processing using RealityCapture, Autodesk Recap Pro and open-source tools, and supported the creation of interactive visualisations for conservation and education.
Point clouds RTK Heritage

Resilience
Contributed to the final phase of an Integrated Climate Change Adaptation (CCA) and Disaster Risk Reduction (DRR) project by synthesising system dynamics modelling, spatial risk mapping and traditional knowledge. The output was a report that helps cities align climate adaptation, disaster governance and local practices in one framework.
